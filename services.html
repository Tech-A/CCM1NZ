Services

Our Services

1NZED is committed to providing high quality and professional immigration services to our clients. Our experienced senior licenced immigration adviser has a thorough understanding of New Zealand's immigration laws and regulations and can provide expert guidance on a range of immigration matters.

Our fees for immigration services are competitive and reflect the time and expertise required to assist with your immigration needs. We offer a range of services, including assistance with work visas, student visas, residence visas, and citizenship applications.



Here is a breakdown of our fees for the various immigration services we offer:


Work Visa Applications:

Initial assessment and advice: $300 - $500

Preparation and submission of work visa application: $500 - $2000

If outside scope of contract, assistance and representation: $150 per hour

Student Visa Applications:

Initial assessment and advice: $200 -$500

Preparation and submission of student visa application: $300-$1000

If outside scope of contract, assistance and representation: $150 per hour

Residence Visa Applications:

Initial assessment and advice: $300-$500

Preparation and submission of residence visa application: $1000 - $3000

If outside scope of contract, assistance and representation: $150 per hour

NZ Citizenship Applications:

Initial assessment and advice: $200

Preparation and submission of citizenship application: $750

If outside scope of contract, assistance and representation: $150 per hour

Please note that these fees are a guide only and may vary depending on the complexity of your immigration matter. We offer a free 15 to 30-minute initial assessment to provide a detailed quote for our services based on your specific immigration needs.

If you would like to know more about our fees for immigration services or to book a free initial assessment, please contact us today. Our team of licensed immigration advisers will be happy to assist you with any questions you may have
