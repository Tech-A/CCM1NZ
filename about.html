About


Immigration Adviser

Conrad Martinez, GDNZIA, LIAA
Senior Licensed Immigration Advisor
IAA #20190194



Conrad is a senior licensed immigration adviser with extensive experience in the field of immigration and operating under the sole trading firm CCM Immigration and Consultancy Services.

Conrad has been working in the immigration industry for over 12 years, starting his career as an immigration officer for the New Zealand government. During his time as an immigration officer, Conrad gained valuable knowledge and experience in processing and approving various types of immigration applications, including visitor visas, student visas, work visas and residence visas.

After working as an immigration officer for several years, Conrad decided to become a licensed immigration adviser to further assist individuals and families with their immigration needs. As a licensed immigration adviser, Conrad is able to offer professional and expert advice on a range of immigration matters, including eligibility, documents required, and the immigration process.

Conrad is dedicated to helping his clients navigate the complex immigration system and achieve their immigration goals. He is committed to providing personalized and comprehensive services, ensuring that each of his clients receives the support and guidance they need to succeed.
